Rose Gram

An interactive rose gram synced with Facebook commenting
Post Gram!


Step 1

Log in to Facebook on this website and allow us to post on your News Feed.

Step 2

Click 'Post Gram!' to make a post on your Facebook feed.

Step 3

Wait for people to comment on your post, and follow the link to view your gram!
